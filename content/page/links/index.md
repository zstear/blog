# Hey, I'm Zach Stear

I'm a cybersecurity enthusiast from Illinois. I graduated from Southern Illinois University Carbondale in May 2025 with a degree in Information Technology.

After college, I bought a home server and dove into experimenting with different operating systems, services, and homelab setups. That hands-on tinkering sparked the idea for this blog: a place to track my learning journey and share it with others.

My plan is simple:
- Write detailed vulnerability writeups (researching past CVEs and exploits)
- Document TryHackMe rooms, HackTheBox machines, or other challenges I complete
- Share custom scripts and tools that help streamline my workflow
- Post questions, confusion, and "aha" moments as I go — because learning isn't linear

I don't want this to just be polished summaries. If I stumble on terminology I don't fully get, or have lingering questions about a technique or vuln, I'll write about it. Sharing the process (including the messy parts) helps me solidify knowledge — and hopefully helps someone else too.

I'm excited to track my progress through these posts and see how far I come. Feel free to check out my LinkedIn and Github below.
